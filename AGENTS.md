## About AGENTS.md

`AGENTS.md` is a mirror of this file. Some AI tools (Codex, Cursor, others) read `AGENTS.md` instead of `CLAUDE.md`. Both files stay in sync automatically when `/onboard` runs.

Do not edit `CLAUDE.md` or `AGENTS.md` manually. They will be overwritten during onboarding.
